Public Acts passed in 1895, 59 Victoria session 2, c.1 - c.6.
From Public General Acts, 1895.
---

1. An act to continue various Expiring Laws.

2. An act to grant money for the purpose of certain Local Loans and for other purposes relating to Local Loans.

3. An act for removing doubts as to the Validity of an Act passed by the Parliament of the Dominion of Canada respecting the Deputy-Speaker of the Senate.

4. An act to re-enact section thirteen of the Purchase of Land (Ireland) Act 1891.

5. An act to provide for the Acquisition of a Site for Public Offices in Westminster and for purposes connected therewith.

6. An act to apply a sum out of the Consolidated Fund to the service of the year ending on the thirty-first day of March one thousand eight hundred and ninety-six and to appropriate the Supplies granted in this Session of Parliament.
