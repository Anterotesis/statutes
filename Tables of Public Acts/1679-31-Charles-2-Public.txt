Public acts passed in 1679, 31 Charles 2 c.1 - c.3.
From Pickering, Statutes At Large, vol 8.
---

1. An act for granting a supply to his Majesty of two hundred and six thousand, four hundred sixty-two pounds, seventeen shillings and three pence, for paying off and disbanding the forces raised since the nine and twentieth of September 1677.

2. An act for the better securing the liberty of the subject, and for prevention of imprisonments beyond the seas.

3. An act for reingrossing of the records of fines burnt or lost in the late fire in the Temple.
