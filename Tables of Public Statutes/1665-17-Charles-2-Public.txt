Public Acts passed in 1665, 17 Charles 2 c.1 - c.9.
From Pickering, Statutes At Large, vol 8; and Ruffhead, Statutes At Large, vol 3.
---

1. An act for granting the sum of twelve hundred and fifty thousand pounds to the King's majesty for his present further supply.

2. An act for restraining nonconformists from inhabiting in corporations.

3. An act for uniting churches in cities and towns corporate.

4. An act for continuance of a former act for regulating the press.

5. An act for attainting Thomas Daleman, Joseph Bampfield, and Thomas Scot, of High Treason, if they render not themselves by a Day.

6. An act for taking away of damage cleer.

7. An act for a more speedy and effectual Proceeding up on Distresses and Avowries for Rents.

8. An act for avoiding unnecessary suits and delays.

9. An act for granting one month's assessment to his Majesty.
