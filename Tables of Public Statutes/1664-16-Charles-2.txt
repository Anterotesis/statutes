Acts passed in 1664, 16 Charles 2 c.1 - c.8.
From Danby Pickering, Statutes At Large, vol 8; and Ruffhead, Statutes At Large, vol 3.
---

1. An Act for the Assembling and Holding of Parliaments once in three Years at the least; and for the Repeal of an Act intituled, An Act for the preventing of Inconveniences happening by the long Intermission of Parliaments.

2. An Act for preventing of Abatement of Writs of Error upon Judgments in the Exchequer.

3. An Act for collecting the Duty arising by Hearth-Money, by Officers to be appointed by his Majesty.

4. An Act to prevent and suppress seditious Conventicles.

5. An Act to prevent the Disturbances of Seamen and others, and to preserve the Stores belonging to his Majesty's Navy Royal.

6. An Act to prevent the delivering up of Merchants Ships.

7. An Act against deceitful, disorderly, and excessive Gaming.

8. An Act for Continuance of a former Act for Regulating the Press.
