Public Acts passed in 1714, 1 George 1, Sess. 1, c.1 - c.3.
From Danby Pickering, Statutes At Large, vol. 13.
---

1. An act for the better support of his Majesty's household and of the honour and dignity of the crown of Great Britain.

2. An act for rectifying mistakes in the names of the commissioners for the land-tax for the year 1714 and for raising so much as is wanting to make up the sum of fourteen hundred thousand pounds, intended to be raised by a lottery for the publick service in the said year.

3. An act to enable persons now residing in Great Britain, to take the oaths, and do all other acts in Great Britain, requisite to qualify themselves to continue their respective places, offices and employments in Ireland.
