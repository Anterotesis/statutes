Public acts passed in 1676-7, 29 & 30 Charles 2 c.1 - c.2.
From Danby Pickering, Statutes At Large, vol 8.
---

1. An act for raising money by a poll, and otherwise, to enable his Majesty to enter into an actual war against the French King, and for prohibiting several French commodities.

2. An act for continuance of two former acts for preventing of theft and rapine upon the northern borders of England.
