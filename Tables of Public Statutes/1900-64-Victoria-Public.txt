Public Acts passed in 1900, 64 Victoria c.1 - c.2.
From Public General Acts, 1901.
---

1. An act to provide for raising a further Supplemental Loan for the service of the year ending the thirty-first day-of March nineteen hundred and one.

2. An act to apply a sum out of the Consolidated Fund to the service of the year ending on the thirty-first day of March one thousand nine hundred and one, and to appropriate the Supplies granted in this Session of Parliament.
