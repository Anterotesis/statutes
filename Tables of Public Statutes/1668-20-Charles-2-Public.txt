Acts passed in 1668, 20 Charles 2 c.1 - c.8.
From Danby Pickering, Statutes At Large, vol 8.
---

1. For raising three hundred and ten thousand pounds, by an imposition on wines and other liquors.

2. For the better payment of monies received for the use of the crown.

3. For the increase and preservation of timber within the forest of Dean.

4. For proceeding to judgment on writs of error brought in the exchequer.

5. For giving liberty to buy and export leather, and skins tanned or dressed.

6. To regulate the trade of silk-throwing.

7. An additional act against the importation of foreign cattle.

8. For the taxing and assessing of the lands of the adventurers within the great level of the fens.
