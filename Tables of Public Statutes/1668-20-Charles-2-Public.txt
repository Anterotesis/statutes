Public Acts passed in 1668, 20 Charles 2 c.1 - c.8.
From Pickering, Statutes At Large, vol 8.
---

1. An act for raising three hundred and ten thousand pounds, by an imposition on wines and other liquors.

2. An act for the better payment of monies received for the use of the crown.

3. An act for the increase and preservation of timber within the forest of Dean.

4. An act for proceeding to judgment on writs of error brought in the exchequer.

5. An act for giving liberty to buy and export leather, and skins tanned or dressed.

6. An act to regulate the trade of silk-throwing.

7. An additional act against the importation of foreign cattle.

8. An act for the taxing and assessing of the lands of the adventurers within the great level of the fens.
