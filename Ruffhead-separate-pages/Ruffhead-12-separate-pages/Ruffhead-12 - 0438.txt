4 other Persons employed in burning Fen Lands, in order to their being converted into Tillage, that the

4 Banks lying contiguous to such Lands are burnt and damaged, by the Fire being suffered to remain too

one or more Justice or Justices of the Peace for the County or Place where such Offence shall be com-

mitted, not interested therein (which Oath such Justice or Justices is and are hereby impowered and re-

quired to administer, and also to hear and determine the said Offence), shall give such Recompence and
