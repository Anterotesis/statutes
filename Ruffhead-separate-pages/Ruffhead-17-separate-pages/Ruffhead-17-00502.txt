XXXVIII. And be it further enacted, That every Person enrolled by virtue of this Act, and entered

into any Regiment or Independent Company of his Majesty’s Army, shall be liable to serve therein during

the Continuance of the present War, and for the Space of one Calendar Month after the End of the War,

if the Regiment or Independent Company in which such Person shall be serving shall be in Great Britain,

or otherwise for the Space of one Calendar Month next after their Arrival in Great Britain; and that every

such Person entering into the Service of the Navy, shall be liable to serve therein during the Continuance of

the present War, and for the Space of one Calendar Month after the End of the War, if the Ship on board

which such Persons shall serve shall be in any of the Ports of Great Britain, or otherwise for the Space of

one Calendar Month next after the Arrival of such Ship in any such Port.
