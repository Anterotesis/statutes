XXVIII. And be it enacted by the Authority aforesaid, That it shall and may be lawful for the Mayor

and Justices of the said Borough, and they are hereby enjoined and required at the General Quarter-fef-

fions of the Peace to be held for the said Borough, as often as they shall fee Occasion, to order, direct,

regulate, settle, and ascertain, the Rates and Wages of Porters, Carters, and Draymen, employed in

carrying any Kind of Goods for Hire, from the Quays to any Part of the said Borough, and also from

One Part of the said Borough to any other Part of the fame; and the Rates and Prices so fixed, to cause

the fame to be hung up in some public Place in the said Borough, to which all Persons may resort for

their Information; and that no Porter, Carman, or Drayman, shall take for Carriage of any Goods or

other Things, above the Rates and Prices so set, upon Pain to forfeit, for every such Offence, the Sum

of Two Shillings, to be levied by Distress and Sale of the Goods or Chattels of the Offender or Offen-

ders, by Warrant of any One or more Justice or Justices of the Peace for the said Borough; and that

the said Mayor and Justices may, in like Manner, order and direct what Kind of Carriages or Drays

the said Porters, Carmen, and Draymen, shall make use of, in drawing Goods through the Streets of the

said Borough; and no Persons shall act as Porters, Carmen, or Draymen, within the said Borough,

but such only as are approved of by the said Mayor and Justices, and are licenced by them, and who shall

have proper Marks to distinguish and (how that they are licenced; and upon the Misbehaviour of any

such Porter, Carman, or Drayman, the said Mayor and Justices, or any One or more of them, are here-

by impowered to revoke such Licence; and in case any such Carmen, Draymen, or Porters, shall carry

any Goods through any of the Streets, Lanes, or Passages of the said Borough, without having first ob-

tained such Licence; or in case such Carmen, Draymen, or Porters, shall carry any such Goods as afore-

said, after their Licence shall have been so revoked as aforesaid, such Carmen, Draymen, or Porters, shall

forfeit, for every such Offence, the Sum of Five Shillings; or if the said Carmen, Draymen, or Porters,

shall use any other Carriages or Drays than what shall be approved of by the said Mayor and Justices ;

such Carmen and Draymen shall, for every such Offence, forfeit the Sum of Five Shillings, to be levied

by Distress and Sale of the Goods and Chattels of the Offender or Offenders, by Warrant of any Ono

or more of the Just ice or Justices of the said Borough.
