An Act for making the River Cham, alias Grant, in the County of Cambridge more navigable from Clay-Hithe-Ferry, to the Queen's-Mill, in the University and Town of Cambridge.

[Preamble.]

Whereas there was heretofore a navigable Passage for Barges, Boats, Lighters, and other Vessels of Burthen, from the Univeristy and Town of Cambridge, along the River Cham, alias Grant, to the River Owze, and so down to the Town and Harbour of Lynn-Regis, in the County of Norfolk; the which hath been of great use to the Counties thereunto adjacent, improvement of Navigation, and most convenient and necessary to and for the said
