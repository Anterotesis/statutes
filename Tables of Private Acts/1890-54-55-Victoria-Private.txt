Private Acts passed in 1890-1, 54 & 55 Victoria c.1-2.
From The Law Reports Public General Statutes, vol. 28.
---

1. An Act to enable the Trustees of the Will of Philip William Flower deceased to postpone the sale and conversion of certain Real Estates held on the trusts of his Will and give them powers with reference thereto.

2. An Act to confer powers on the Executors and Trustees of the Will of the late Thomas Andrew Walker in relation to his real and personal estate and various contracts entered into by him for the execution of Public Works.
