Private Acts passed in 1912, 2 & 3 George 5 c.1 - c.2.
From The Law Reports Public General Statutes, vol. 50.
---

1. An act to authorise the Trustees of the will of John Howard deceased to transfer to a company with limited liability to be hereafter formed the business of the testator and certain lands and premises held in connection therewith and for other purposes.

2. An act to dissolve the marriage of Shenstone John Bishop of 20 Merrion Square in the city of Dublin surgeon-dentist with Ethel Mally Bishop his now wife and to enable him to marry again and for other purposes.
