Private Acts passed in 1920, 10 & 11 George 5 c.1 - c.7.
From The Local and Private Acts, 1920.
---

2. An act to dissolve the marriage of Jose Baroness Carbery of 5 Chester Place London in the County of London with John Baron Carbery of Castle Freke in the County of Cork Ireland and to enable her to marry again and for other purposes.
