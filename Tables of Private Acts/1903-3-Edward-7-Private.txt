Private Acts passed in 1903, 3 Edward 7 c.1 - c.2.
From The Law Reports Public General Statutes, vol. 41.
---

1. An Act to vest the estate of Lochnell in the county of Argyll in trustees for certain purposes with power of sale and other powers.

2. An Act to enable money to be raised or secured upon the Pentillie estate in the county of Cornwall devised by the will of Augustus Coryton esquire deceased.
