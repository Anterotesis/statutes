Acts passed in 1896, 59 & 60 Victoria c.1 - c.59.
From Public General Acts, 1896.
---

1. An Act to continue temporarily certain Powers for the Removal of Difficulties at Elections under the Local Government Act, 1894.

2. An Act to provide, during twelve months, for the Discipline and Regulation of the Army.

3. An Act to apply certain sums out of the Consolidated Fund to the service of the years ending on the thirty-first day of March one thousand eight hundred and ninety-six, and one thousand eight hundred and ninety-seven.
