Private Acts passed in 1907, 7 Edward 7 c.1-7.
From The Law Reports Public General Statutes, vol. 45.
---

1. An act to dissolve the marriage of Charles Edward Galwey a Captain in the Royal Irish Regiment with Annie Louise Galwey his now wife and to enable him to marry again and for other purposes. (Galwey's Divorce.)

2. An act to dissolve the marriage of Henry Morgan Byrne barrister-at-law with Edith Laura Byrne his now wife and to enable him to marry again and for other purposes. (Byrne's Divorce.)

3. An act to authorise Raymond Hill Baines to assume and bear the Christian names of Henry Rodd in lieu of the Christian names of Raymond Hill. (Baines Name.)

4. An act to remove doubts as to the validity of a certain decree dated thirtieth October one thousand eight hundred and ninety-three of the High Court of Justice (Probate Divorce and Admiralty Division) dissolving the marriage solemnized on the sixteenth day of November one thousand eight hundred and eighty between William Howard Murphy Grimshaw and Mary Evalina Julia Murphy Grimshaw then Mary Evalina Julia Woodward spinster and to confirm the said decree. (Murphy Grimshaw's Divorce (Validation).)

5. An act to dissolve the marriage of Lida Eleanor Purcell FitzGerald with Gerald Purcell FitzGerald her present husband and to enable her to marry again and for other purposes. (FitzGerald's Divorce.)

6. An act to dissolve the marriage of Gwenllian Pascoe Killery the wife of St. John Brown Killery a Captain in the Royal Army Medical Corps with the said St. John Brown Killery and to enable her to marry again and for other purposes. (Killery's Divorce.)

7. An act to declare legitimate certain children of Thomas Hamilton Sabine Pasley. (Pasley Children Legitimisation.)
