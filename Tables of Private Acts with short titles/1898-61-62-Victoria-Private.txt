Private Acts passed in 1898, 61 & 62 Victoria c.1 - c.3.
From The Law Reports Public General Statutes, vol. 35.
---

1. An act to confer further powers on the Executors and Trustees of the Will of the late Thomas Andrew Walker in relation to his real and personal estate. (Walker's Estate.)

2. An act to dissolve the Marriage of Edith Susan Anna Vernon Hart the wife of Henry Chichester Hart with the said Henry Chichester Hart and to enable her to marry again and for other purposes. (Bart's Divorce.)

3. An act to dissolve the Marriage of Cliffe Henry Vigors of the New Barracks Limerick a Captain in the Royal Irish Regiment with Katharine Mary Vigors his now wife and to enable him to marry again and for other purposes. (Vigors' Divorce.)
