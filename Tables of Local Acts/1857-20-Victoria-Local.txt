Local Acts passed in 1857, 20 Victoria c.1 - c.3.
From A Collection of the Public General Statutes, 1857.
---

1. An act for enabling the Great Western, Bristol and Exeter, and South Devon Railway Companies to afford further Assistance towards the Completion of the Cornwall Railway between Plymouth and Truro; for extending the Time for the Completion thereof; and for other Purposes.

2. An act to re-incorporate Price's Patent Candle Company, Limited, and for other Purposes.

3. An act to enable the Whitehaven, Cleator, and Egremont Railway Company to raise additional Capital; and for other Purposes.
