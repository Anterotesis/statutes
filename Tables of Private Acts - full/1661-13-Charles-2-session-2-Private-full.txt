Private Acts passed in 1661, 13 Charles 2 session 2 c.1 - c.5.
From Danby Pickering, Statutes At Large, vol. 8; and Ruffhead, Statutes At Large, vol. 3.
---

1. For Confirming An Act for Restoring of the Marquess of Hertford in the Dukedom of Somerset.

2. For dividing Trinity Church in Kingston upon Hull from Hasle.

3. To enable Algeron Peyton Doctor of Divinity, to make Sale of Part of his Lands for Payment of Debts, &c.

4. For Confirming An Act for Restitution of Thomas Earl of Arundell, Surrey and Norfolk, to the Dignity and Title of Duke of Norfolk.

5. For Confirming of private Acts.
